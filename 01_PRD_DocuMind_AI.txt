Product Overview1.1 Product VisionDocuMind AI is an AI-powered platform that transforms how businesses create documentation. By leveraging the Gemini API, it converts raw business ideas and process descriptions into professional, structured documents within minutes.1.2 Mission StatementTo democratize professional business and software documentation by making it fast, intelligent, and accessible to every business — regardless of size or technical expertise.1.3 Problem Being SolvedBusiness owners cannot communicate software requirements effectively to developers.Creating BRD, SRS, and related documents takes 2–5 days of manual effort.Small businesses cannot afford dedicated Business Analysts.Incomplete requirements lead to miscommunication, rework, and increased cost.Students and freelancers lack access to industry-standard documentation tools.2. Core Features3.1 Project ManagementCreate and manage multiple projects.Each project has a dedicated workspace with full history.View all previous reports and documents per project.3.2 AI-Guided Business AnalysisUser describes their business in natural language.AI (Gemini) asks intelligent follow-up questions to gather complete context.AI identifies pain points, automation opportunities, and recommends solutions.AI generates a structured business analysis from the collected data.3.3 Document GenerationBusiness DocumentsExecutive SummaryBusiness Analysis ReportSWOT AnalysisGap AnalysisRisk AnalysisGrowth StrategySoftware DocumentsBusiness Requirements Document (BRD)Software Requirements Specification (SRS)User StoriesTest Cases3.4 Export &amp; DownloadExport documents in PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and Excel formats.Preview documents before downloading.Download individual or bundled document packages.3.5 AI Chat on ReportsUsers can chat with AI about any generated report.Ask follow-up questions, request clarifications, or generate additional sections.3.6 AuthenticationJWT-based secure authentication.Free tier — single user role.4. User Flows4.1 New Project FlowUser registers / logs in.User creates a new project (name, industry, brief description).User enters detailed business information in the intake form.AI asks clarifying follow-up questions in a chat-style interface.User answers questions; AI processes all inputs.AI generates a Business Analysis Report.User selects which documents to generate.User previews generated documents.User downloads in preferred format.4.2 Return User FlowUser logs in and views dashboard with all projects.User selects existing project.User views previous reports and documents.User can chat with AI about any existing report.User can regenerate or export new formats.5. Non-Functional Requirements5.1 PerformanceDocument generation should complete within 60 seconds for standard projects.API response time &lt; 2 seconds for non-AI endpoints.5.2 ScalabilityBackend built with FastAPI for async, high-performance request handling.PostgreSQL database with proper indexing for query performance.5.3 SecurityJWT tokens with expiry and refresh mechanism.All API endpoints protected by authentication middleware.User data isolated per account.5.4 UsabilityIntuitive UI — non-technical users should complete first document generation without training.Mobile-responsive design.6.
